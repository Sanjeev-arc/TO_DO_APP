Our Story

Founded in 2020, To-Do Master began as a simple idea: create a task management tool that actually works for real people. We noticed that most productivity apps were either too complex or too basic, leaving users frustrated and unproductive.

Our team of productivity enthusiasts, developers, and designers came together to build something different—an app that combines powerful features with an intuitive interface. Today, we help thousands of users worldwide stay organized and achieve their goals.
